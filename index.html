Sit, Boy! Research Initiative

Greetings from the University of Kentucky! Thank you for visiting our site.

Why am I here?

You are here to play a game.

What will you do?

In this study, you will play two different games. The first game has a pixelated dog that will cycle through randomized animations including stand, walk, run, lick, and sit. You are to press the REWARD button as soon as you see the dog sit. The game will run for 30 seconds, and the dog will sit 5 times total within the 30 seconds. The second game is a target practice game that will run for 30 seconds and require you to hit the target as they appear, also as quickly as possible.

Who is working on this project?

This project is part of a collaboration between the University of Kentucky (UKY) and North Carolina State University (NCSU) Computer Science Departments. It is run by a graduate student in the University of Kentucky Computer Science Department.
